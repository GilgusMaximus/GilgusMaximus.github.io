Treasure Cave

Description

Treasure Cave is an active mobile mining game.

The gameplay consists of scanning the surrounding rocks for rare treasures and then mining these. In order to turn around, you need to rotate your phone !

But beware the dangers of the mine, like hidden dynamite or rising water levels !

Current version: 1.3 pre Release

Goals of the project

The game was created in a team effort during the TUM SemesterGameJam 2018 Winter Edition. The theme of said gamejam was “Discovery”, so we decided to go for discovering treasures !

So the first goal was to submit a game to the gamejam. We reached this goal, and even won the category price “Engineering” !

The next major goal is to create a complete mobile game and release the finished version on the Google Play store.

The main learning goals (post gamejam) are to get into the publishing process of an application and take first steps into marketing of a product !

Roadmap

In order to release a version of the game on the play store, the game has to offer more content. At the moment there are only 2 story levels and the challenge mode.

Therefore the next milestones are creating a consens of all features we want to implement, adding these features, and polishing the game, as well as getting started with marketing.

For more on Treasure Cave, follow me on Twitter, or take a look on our gamejam site over at itch.io, and download the gamejam version !
