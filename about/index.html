About Me

I’m Luca Hohmann. I study Informatik: Games Engineering at the Technische Universität München in my fourth semester. Currently I’m working as Hiwi for the Chair of Information Systems.

My field of interests are Procedural Generation, Augmented Reality, Privacy & Security, Open Source as well as Game Development in general.

In my free time I love to go biking. I also enjoy attending local events like workshops, meetups or talks. But most importantly I love reading textbooks and playing games.
