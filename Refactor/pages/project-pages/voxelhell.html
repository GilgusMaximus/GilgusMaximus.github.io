Voxel Hell

Description

Voxel Hell is a fast paced bullet hell game.

The gameplay is based around the classic bullet hell genre. Try to survive as long as possible, and make as much points as possible. And try to beat the highscores of other players !

But there’s a twist. The longer you play, the smaller the playzone becomes !

Current version: v1.1

Goals of the project

The game was created by a 2 person team during the Ludum Dare 42 gamejam. The theme of the jam was “Runnng out of Space”, so a bullet hell game with the additional twist was a reasonable idea. Because we wanted to finish our first good jam game at this Ludum Dare, the main goal was to make a small but enjoyable project, which should be polished after the 72 hours. The main goal was to improve our workflow and creation process for future gamejams !
